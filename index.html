What is this app?

This tool splits the monthly AC bill in the fairest way possible — based on exactly when each person was in the hostel, down to the minute.

How do I log my time?

For each day, enter the time you came in and the time you left:

  • Away all day — leave both fields empty
  • Here all day — from 00:00 to 24:00
  • Partial stay — e.g. from 14:00 to 22:00
  • Overnight — e.g. from 22:00 to 06:00 (next morning)

How is the bill split? (The fairest method)

Instead of just adding up hours, the app looks at every single minute of the month and checks who was in the hostel at that exact minute.

  1. For each minute of the month, the app counts how many people were present.
  2. If you were the only person in the hostel at a given minute, you pay the full cost of that minute.
  3. If 2 people were in the hostel at the same minute, they split the cost of that minute equally.
  4. If 3 people, they split three ways — and so on.

Example

Person A is in the hostel from 12 AM to 9 AM. Person B is in the hostel from 9 PM to 9 AM (next morning).

  • From 12 AM to 9 AM — both are present, so they split the cost for those 9 hours equally.
  • From 9 AM to 9 PM — only Person B is present, so Person B pays the full cost for those 12 hours.

This is the most fair because you only pay for the time you were actually using the AC, and when multiple people share the space, they share the cost.
