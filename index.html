What is this app?

This tool helps split the monthly AC bill in a fair way — based entirely on how many hours each person actually stayed in the hostel.

How do I log my hours?

  • 0 hours — you were away the whole day (out of the hostel)
  • 24 hours — you stayed the entire day
  • Any number in between (e.g. 12, 8.5) — you were here for part of the day

How is the bill split?

Here's how it works, step by step:

  1. Everyone's daily hours are added up to get their total monthly hours.
  2. All members' total hours are added together for total hostel hours.
  3. Your share of the bill = (Your hours ÷ Everyone's total hours) × Total AC bill

Example

If you stayed 300 hours, your friend stayed 200 hours, and the total AC bill is Rs. 10,000:

  • Total hours = 500
  • Your share = (300 ÷ 500) × 10,000 = Rs. 6,000
  • Friend's share = (200 ÷ 500) × 10,000 = Rs. 4,000

Everyone pays only for the time they were actually in the hostel. Simple and fair.
